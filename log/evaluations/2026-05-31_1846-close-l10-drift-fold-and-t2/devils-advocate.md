## Bottom line

A competently executed housekeeping session whose apparent strength is inflated by the project's own self-imposed bookkeeping ceremony and by "all green" check runs that only ever tested the no-op path. The L10 *decision* was genuinely well-investigated; the L10 *verification* was not — the divergence-detection branch the tool exists for was never exercised after the rewrite. Both parties treated "small change" as license to skip the spec-edit review and feature-branch discipline they normally apply, and the human's commit/push approvals were choreography more than control. Nothing here broke, and nothing here was hard — which is exactly why a retrospective should resist grading it higher than the modest, low-stakes work it was.
