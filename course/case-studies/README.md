# Case studies

Two worked, real accounts of building this course **with** Claude Code — authentic dogfooding, not
illustrations. Read them alongside the [capstone](../capstone/): both double as exemplar material for
your own end-to-end work.

| Case study | What it shows |
|---|---|
| [How this course was built and is maintained](../capstone/case-study.md) | The intended workflow: spec-driven development, deliberate context engineering, the custom extensions we built and use, and verification as the through-line. The worked exemplar for your capstone. |
| [An honest retrospective of the collaboration](./collaboration-retrospective.md) | The candid twin: what the human↔Claude working relationship actually looked like across 23 build sessions — the strengths to emulate and the recurring failure modes to guard against — assessed by an 11-reviewer self-evaluation panel. |

The first is how the build is *meant* to go; the second is the panel's candid **self-assessment** of
how it *actually* went — warts read in, and flagged as a self-evaluation to weigh rather than take on
trust (see its conflict-of-interest note). Together they give you a real, non-trivial job and an honest
reckoning with how it went: the two things worth studying before your capstone.
